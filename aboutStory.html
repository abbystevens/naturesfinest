About Us

We bring 30+ years of commercial agricultural and business management experience to our latest farm, Nature’s Finest. As a long-time farmer, Ronny applies his expertise and dedication to following best agricultural production practices. Ellen manages the office, administrative, and marketing functions.

After almost 20 years growing vegetables, melons, and citrus in California’s Central San Joaquin Valley, we relocated to the Hood River Valley in December 2012. We chose to name our new farm Nature’s Finest to reflect our intent to deliver the very best produce that our customers deserve.

We were drawn here by the beautiful scenery, excellent farm land, and the growing support for smaller family farms. We are finding the valley to be a great fit for us and look forward to contributing to the local food system for many years.

- Ronny and Ellen Tannenbaum
