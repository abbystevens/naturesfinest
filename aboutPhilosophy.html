Nature’s Finest is dedicated to growing the best vegetables for the local market area. We are mindful of what goes into the land and choose our farming practices accordingly. Our decades of experience in commercial agriculture enable us to meet the needs of our wholesale customers.

Locally Fresh

Our 25 acre farm is in the upper Hood River Valley near Mount Hood. Growing conditions are perfect at the farm - which sits in the fertile valley's "sunbelt". We target local and regional customers to ensure that our vegetables are fresh and delicious. Our produce is hand-picked, cleaned and packed, then delivered straight from our farm to the retailer or restaurant.

Reliable

To provide a full season of fresh produce to our buyers, we start our transplants in our own greenhouse, plant early crops under tunnels, and schedule multiple plantings during the season. Meticulous planning and strategic planting allows us to produce the quantities our customers require. We have zucchini available in May, snap peas for the entire growing season, and sweet corn through October.

Competitive

We believe local produce should be accessible at a reasonable price in local markets, working within the constraints of commercial buyers. We work closely with our customers to determine their needs throughout the season and price our produce competitively as compared to large wholesalers.

Varied

Nature’s Finest grows a wide range of items, supplementing standard crops such as lettuce, broccoli, cucumbers, spinach and zucchini with specialty items like purple cauliflower, brussels sprouts on the stalk, and flower sprouts.
